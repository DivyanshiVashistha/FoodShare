Our Mission

FoodShare is dedicated to eliminating food waste and hunger by connecting generous donors with those in need.

Founded in 2020, we recognized a critical problem: millions of people go hungry every day while perfectly good food goes to waste. We created FoodShare to bridge this gap and build a community where no one has to go to bed hungry.

Through our platform, we've facilitated thousands of food donations, serving communities across the nation. Every meal shared is a step toward ending hunger and building a more compassionate society.
