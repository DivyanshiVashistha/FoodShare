What We Accept

  • Fresh fruits and vegetables
  • Packaged dry goods (rice, pasta, beans)
  • Canned foods (within expiry date)
  • Cooked meals (prepared within 4 hours)
  • Baked goods (fresh)
  • Dairy products (properly refrigerated)
  • Beverages (sealed/unopened)

What We Cannot Accept

  • Expired food items
  • Food with broken seals
  • Home-canned goods
  • Food with signs of spoilage
  • Alcoholic beverages
  • Food requiring temperature control without proper storage
  • Pet food
